music

  • astronomy by conan gray, it's so Emotion and hit me completely out of the blue
  • strawberries and cigarettes by troye sivan, the song for the gay movie 'love, simon', which is also one of my favorite books, and troye sivan is just !!!
  • butterfly by loona, a kpop song about believing in yourself and it's amazing
  • clover by fromis_9, another kpop song about happiness and growing up and dreams and the unknown future and letting go of the past and it's just so mmMMM
  • through and through by khai dreams, atwood remix is one of those songs where if it comes up in shuffle i'll rewind it and play it twice. indie lofi-hiphop ? song about hanging on and being brave, i think.

articles/short stories

really, really cool articles and short stories i've collected. they're all super worth a read! a lot of these were recommended to me by jan likipi so thank you!!

  • the last question by isaac asimov. about entropy and scifi and the end of the world, and it's really really cool.
  • the library of babel by jorge luis borges. less epic than the one above imo but still really good! and kinda scifi too.
  • foxen in the henhice by richard lederer, a really fun take on the english language and how bad english is. a super fun read and i first read this 5 years ago and it stayed in my mind ever since, and i only dug it up again recently.
  • take your time by nicky case. another scifi one, but it's aaaaaa so good! it's about how people are always super rush and capitalism and all that shit. it's amazing.

books

these are just some of the books that sit on the 'favorites' section of my bookshelf, and i love them to bits.

  • autoboyography by christina lauren. a gay kid grrowing up in a highly religious neighbourhood with loving parents and then he falls in love with the son of the bishop. it's that kind of book you devour within a day and leave reeling for weeks.
  • word by word by kory stamper. kory stamper is a lexicographer and this book is about how they make dictionaries but it's soooo fun to read? i learnt so much and like. lexicography sounds PAINFUL and FUN.
  • the hunger games by suzanne collins. if you haven't read this what are you doing? the hunger games is like, my top book of all times. i've read it at least 20 times and counting.
