plurality/multiplicity: about systems

plurality is, essentially, people who are Many, that is, there are more than one individual in their body. i have quite a number of friends who are a part of a system. a lot of people don't know about the existence of these, but they're very important and very much existing!

some more links which will explain things a lot better and also address questions and stuff:

  • more than one, a really good website that tells you about it.
  • power to the plurals, another website with a nice faq.

ableism

ableism is easily the most common form of discrimination around, yet rarely anyone pays attention to it. words such as stupid and insane might sound fine to you, but really, they are Extremely Bad. i urge you to drop them from your vocabulary.

  • ableist terms to avoid by autistic hoya. you might not need to follow this completely, but it's an amazing list and definitely a good thing to keep in mind.
  • i don't have a lot of other links, but if this catches your eye at all, please research and learn more about it! support for disabled folks :)
