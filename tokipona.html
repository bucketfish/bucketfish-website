toki pona!

toki pona is a constructed langauge by sonja lang. i got into it in may 2020 and i've been intergrated in its community ever since.

it's a language of only 120(ish, depending on how you count) words, and therefore extremely easy to learn :)

my stuff

you probably know, but i've made a ton of toki pona stuff :) most of these are not made by me alone:

  • kala pona, my toki pona youtube music channel :D
  • tu kuntu, probably The Longest Work of toki pona (at the moment). written by atawan and me, though names are redacted in here.
  • lipu kule, a publication/newsletterish thing?
  • kulupu pi toki musi, a gaming channel !
  • nasin sitelen Wakalito, a toki pona keyboard for ios. i did the code :)

resources

the internet is a great place and here are some to get started.

  • conlang critic: toki pona by jan misali, an episode about toki pona. it's a great introduction if you wanna learn more about it!
  • ma pona pi toki pona, the best toki pona discord server. it's a super nice and inclusive place! i half live on it entirely. great place.
  • 12 days of toki pona by jan misali. definitely my favorite course out there, but i'm probably biased, since i learnt with this. either way, he explains things really quickly and is a good start.
  • lipu sona pona by /dev/urandom. a good course too!
  • lipu pi ijo pi toki pona by atawan. neat little website.
  • and i'll just link tokipona.org again.
